  • Pickles
  • Ketchup
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Form the ground beef into two equal-sized tightly packed balls.
  2. Heat a cast-iron skillet or griddle over high heat until smoking hot.
  3. Place the beef balls onto the dry skillet and press them down immediately with a heavy spatula until they are completely flat.
  4. Season generously with salt and pepper, then let them sear for about 2 minutes until a deep brown crust forms.
  5. Flip the patties, place a slice of cheese on each, and cook for 1 more minute until the cheese is melted.
  6. Place the patties onto your toasted buns, add your favorite toppings, and enjoy!
