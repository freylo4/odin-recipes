Baguette Recipes

This is the type of Baguette we all want to smash at some point in our life right?? The following recipe is for everyone!!

Ingridients

  • 1 fresh baguette (preferably with sesame seeds or grains)
  • 2 tbsp green basil pesto
  • 1 tbsp balsamic glaze (crema di balsamico)
  • 100g roast beef or premium salami (thinly sliced)
  • 1 ball of fresh mozzarella or camembert (sliced)
  • 1 large tomato (cut into thick slices)
  • ½ cucumber (sliced)
  • A handful of arugula (rocket) or fresh spinach
  • A few fresh parsley leaves
  • Optional toppings: Pickled olives, pepperoncini, and thin red onion rings
  • Seasoning: Salt, coarse black pepper, and dried Italian herbs

Steps

  1. Prepare the baguette: Slice the baguette lengthwise using a bread knife, but don't cut it completely through so it holds the fillings better.
  2. Spread the base: Generously spread the green basil pesto on the bottom half of the baguette.
  3. Layer the meat: Place the thinly sliced roast beef or salami on top of the pesto, ruffling it slightly to create volume.
  4. Add the cheese: Evenly arrange the mozzarella or camembert slices over the meat.
  5. Pack the veggies: Layer the cucumber slices first, followed by the thick tomato slices, and then add your onion rings, olives, or pepperoncini.
  6. Season it up: Season the tomatoes directly with a pinch of salt, pepper, and Italian herbs, then drizzle the balsamic glaze over them.
  7. The greens: Top everything off with a fresh layer of arugula and parsley.
  8. Press & Serve: Carefully press the baguette together, cut it in half if you like, and enjoy your sandwich!
